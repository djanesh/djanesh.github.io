This is the homepage and blog of Janesh Devkota.

Janesh is a water resources engineer currently working in Dynamic Solutions International LLC in Edmonds, WA. He completed his PhD from Auburn University in 2014 and completed masters in Civil Engineering (MCE) from Auburn University in Summer 2012. During his PhD period in Auburn, he worked on number of projects that required numerical modeling, data analysis, data munging and data visualization. He is a passionate scientist who loves explaining data science / data visualization to non-technical business audiences.

Data science related courses that I have taken in grad school can be found here. To deepen by data science skills, I enjoy taking online courses. I have taken more than 10 online courses with Hadoop, Spark, R, Python, SQL, Tableu etc.
